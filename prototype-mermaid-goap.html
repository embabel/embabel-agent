Header diagram: single-row current step

Designed for the sticky header: readable at low height, with one active stage and only essential state.

flowchart LR
  request["Research request"] --> categorize["Categorize"]
  categorize --> research["GPT + Claude research"]
  research --> merge["Merge named reports"]
  merge --> critique["Critique"]
  critique --> decision{"Condition"}
  decision -->|satisfactory| accept["Accept report"]
  decision -. unsatisfactory .-> redo["Redo research"]
  redo -. replaces reports .-> merge
